VaxScout

Scout is your friendly neighborhood bot, sniffing out available COVID-19 vaccination appointments in several states 24-7.

Finding available appointments for patients in need - without constantly hitting "refresh"

With multiple vaccine providers and websites that aren't always user-friendly, locating available COVID-19 vaccine appointments can be an uphill battle. VaxScout is here to help. We automatically locate available appointments and post them to Twitter. All you have to do is enable notifications on tweets for the VaxScout in your state, and you'll be notified whenever we find something!
